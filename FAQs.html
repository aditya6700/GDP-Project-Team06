Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: How can I donate blood?

A: To donate blood, you can visit our nearest blood donation center during their operating hours. Our friendly staff will guide you through the process.

Q: Can I request blood for a specific blood type?

A: Yes, you can request blood for a specific blood type. Please fill out our blood request form, and we'll do our best to match your request with a compatible donor.
